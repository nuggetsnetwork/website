Experiencing Parenthood

First Time Parent*

If you are reading this then you must have just been blessed with a new born or you are here to know about some tips regarding managing a new born. If its your first time being a parent then you must already been going through lot of dilemma about managing your little one. While there will be lots of suggestions and tips for different problems of baby. There is always a chance that it may or not apply to your baby. One thing to note is that every baby will have different needs and things that might differ based on various environmental circumstances. Could be from where you are living to what the condition of the baby is when she is born. So below tips are meant to be for general reference.There is just no one rule that works for every baby. Dont read too much or take too many suggestions. Know and believe that as parents you will find your own way to handle your baby. That is the speciality of parenthood. No one is an expert from beginning and you will learn your way into it. So if somethings are not working dont presume that you are bad parents. There is nothing called bad parent. Know that you will eventually figure your own way of managing your baby and eventually become an expert in managing your baby. Trust me when I say that after a few months no one else will be able to manage your baby than you both.

Baby Milestones

1. Baby birth weight milestone. This is an important milestone for baby to ensure baby is being fed well and her growth is p rogressing normally. During initial first two weeks after baby is born frequent weight checks are done at hospital to check the baby weight is increasing gradually or not. Especially babies tend to lose weight in the first few days of their birth when they initially start feeding. So if the babies weight is 3 kg at the time of birth. Next few days the baby weight might come down during which the doctor might ask you to continue visiting them every few days to continue monitoring the babies condition until the point when the babies weight comes back to the birth weight. So this point when the babies weight reaches back to the birth weight is the milestone that determines baby is back to healthy routine and also feeding is proper. So its important for especially feeding mothers to consult lactation expert to review if the baby is feeding well or if the baby is not gaining weight. Apparently although it might appear baby is feeding well but there could be case where baby is actually not getting fed well. Lactation consultant will review how the baby is breast feeding and suggest appropriate corrections that will eliminate this cause. 2. Jaundice: TSH levels drop to normal levels. Kindly note that the TSH normal levels are different for different days of baby age. So it’s important to consult doctors to understand the normal levels. Else you could be mislead that TSH is abnormal. The same reading could be normal for a baby on x days of age and can be abnormal on x days. 3rd Month: Started Cooing and making sounds in response Watching your movements and increased eye contact Smiling gets more evident Had some terrible episodes of pain. Root cause usually isn’t clear but it would appear as if the baby is in pain. A second she smiles and suddenly surging episode of crying continues which is unsettling. Common causes of it could be digestion issues, Gas, Cold causing nasal and throat blockages that could also effect and cause ear pain. Also started to grab things with more accuracy. Hand and Eye co-ordination improves. If in Play Gym she would now be able to reach to items more easily. Crying near strangers and new environments. Recognising parents. Jaundice Its common for new borns to have Jaundice during their earlier birth days. Usually jaundice subsides in few days after birth. But its important to monitor the TSH levels in first few days of birth and take sufficient treatment if the TSH levels are high.

How to check Jaundice without test?

Visual indication of babies body and eyes turning yellow gradually.Touching the baby on her chest area with fingers would usually show yellow patches that would indicate jaundice.

Treatment for Jaundice

Overnight blue light exposure and then monitoring the TSH levels further.Kindly note that TSH might be slightly more when monitored with infra device immediately after the blue light exposure. So in such cases based on doctors recommendation a blood sample would be calculated to determine the exact TSH levels of the baby to consider further treatment.In worst circumstances baby will need to be admitted into NICU for continued treatment of Jaundice.

Reasons baby Cry

Feeding - Usual tendency of babies is to get fed every few hours during their initial days of birth. So monitoring baby feed timings is essential to understand baby feeding patterns and negate this need of baby that might cause the baby crying. You can notice baby trying to suck or her lips eager to suck things are indications of baby being hungry. Lack of Sleep - Gas - Burping after sleeping is very essential. Giving tummy time once in a while can also help with gas relief. Pain - Any other discomfort. Temperature - Temperature of the surroundings and the clothing can also be a reason. Newbornes are usually expected to feel better when they are warm.

Burping Techniques

Over the shoulder traditional way of burping. Patting on babies back lightly or just rubbing the babies back in circular motions over the shoulder. Patting babies back in a stomach position. Rubbing the babies back in a sitting position with her chin held in your arm. Make sure you are not holding the neck that might cause choke and breathing difficulty.

Colic Relief or Colic cry

This is one of the reasons where we don’t know why babies cry. They just cry continuously where reason is not evident. Scientifically colic could have multiple reasons. Babies digestive system getting accommodated to the new routine of diet. Her stomach is still growing so this could be reason to form and adjust to excessive feeding causing discomfort. Additional Gas that baby accumulates during feeding or crying etc.Colic drops from doctor. Calming Techniques As I mentioned at the beginning of the blog every baby is different. You can just try any of these and figure out what works best for your little one. Below are few things that worked for us. • Swaddle is important most of times. Babies tend to easily wake up during sleep sessions especially when their arms are free they jerk suddenly during sleeps and swaddle ensures this doesn’t happen and absorbs most of the movements during sleep and ensures babies sleep is not disturbed frequently. • Low light/Dim light room with calm environment. • White Noise sounds does help in multiple ways. One way it gives baby a calming feel. Other ways especially when there are loud noises or any other noise that disturbs baby white noise near baby negates those voices and ensures baby doesn’t get disturbed by sudden noises. • Singing for baby or sh sh or hissing sounds can also calm babies. Few more tips • Holding her in vertical position in arms keeps her calm. • Feeding is the only thing that makes her quiet. Pacifier sometimes helps but not always. • Rocking her in slanted position helps sometimes. We use our detachable trolley seat to rock her. • Slight massage right before her bath around in the evening. Feed her and then get her to sleep. Bathing has definitely been observed to give her sound sleep in nights and has increased her sleep durations in the nights as well. Sickness • Cold or Nose Block or Breathing Issues - Nasal drops, Nasal spray, baby nasal aspirator. For any of these first contact your pediatrician.
